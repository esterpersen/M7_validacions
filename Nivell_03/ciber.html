HOW IT WORKS

THEORY

Dive into the polynesian culture. Learn the basic concepts and steps, and up your level with every lesson!

PRACTICE

Put in practice what you've learned and improve your technique, resistance and flexibility.

TEST

At the end of each lesson, test your skills, and get feedback from a professional dancer.


WHY START A COURSE

Flexibility

Learn at your own peace and wherever you like. You just need 2m2 and motivation!

Professional tips

Submit your test videos and get feedback and tips from a professional dancer to progress.

Health

Dancing is an excellent way of maintaining both your physical and mental health in great shape.

Knowledge

Get into the polynesian culture and learn all about their traditions, as well as how they express through dancing.

Network

We have a discord channel where we share interesting content, and share our tips. Socialize with dancers from around the globe.
